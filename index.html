#1 . Enrolment

You join TYP community and fellowship program

#2 . Mentorship

As part of TYP community, you attend internal community events with students like you who have the hunger to learn. As part of fellowship program, you are taught LIVE by mentors with proven experience in tech industry with Tier-1 companies on our online platform.

#3 . Project Work

You are assited by mentors to complete a presentable project in team, also learning about tech collaboration and planning tools like GitHub projects, DevOps and OSS contribution.

#4 . Reporting

You create presentation and execution report just like in real tech companies about your work and submit it for assessment. These project informations are also shared to the companies as your work portfolio.

#5 . Assessment

A TYP mentor assess your report, helps you make it better and approves you for certification once satisfied.

#6 . Certification & Showcase

You earn a certificate mentioning your work, remark from mentors and your profile is showcased to our employer network.
